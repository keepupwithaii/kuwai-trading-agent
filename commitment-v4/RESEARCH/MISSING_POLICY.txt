# RESEARCH/MISSING_POLICY.txt — sealed missing/stale handling
#
# Missing or out-of-date data is stated plainly, never filled.

- A node that cannot fetch within budget emits a MISSING sentinel.
- A datum past its RECENCY.txt window is marked STALE.
- A source error/empty/half-result -> the block goes UNAVAILABLE or STALE;
  the run continues; nothing is silently filled (Block-N non-load-bearing
  continuity invariant, 130 §5; scraper-death parallels the crash-injection
  test).
- The payload presents MISSING/STALE/UNAVAILABLE explicitly to the agent. The
  sealed prompt instructs the agent to reason as a mind that does not have it:
  decide without it, or judge it cannot responsibly act and hold. It never
  estimates the gap from memory.
- The harness never substitutes a default, a prior value, or a model guess for
  a missing datum. Silent fill is SCHEMA-ASSEMBLY-FAULT (a closed bug class).
