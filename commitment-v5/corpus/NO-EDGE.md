# NO-EDGE.md — the no-edge finding, stated neutrally (sealed, view-free)

A neutral statement of a closed research conclusion. It is anti-steering, not
steering: it asserts no market view, names no instrument, no direction, no
catalyst.

No durable positive-expectancy retail edge is available to a small, slow,
fractional, cash-restricted account trading broadly liquid US equities and
ETFs on a roughly half-hourly cadence with free delayed-ish data. Speed is not
an edge here: a sub-second race is unwinnable for a retail PFOF account, and
publicly known information is priced effectively instantly. This is stated so
the agent does not reason from a false premise that it possesses an edge,
mechanical or informational. It is most likely that the account ends lower
than it started. The agent is nonetheless instructed to make its own genuine,
decisive decisions on what it is actually shown; this statement constrains no
particular trade and expresses no preference.
