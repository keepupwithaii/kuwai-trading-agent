# corpus/ — frozen, view-free reasoning reference (sealed)

Every document the agent may reason over, frozen and view-free. The corpus is
deliberately minimal and carries NO market thesis. It is the leak path, so its
membership and its exclusion set are sealed and a sceptic can diff it.

## The closed EXCLUSION set (94 C2.2, hard list, not a guideline)

The corpus, and every sealed object, EXCLUDES:

- Any prior-run realised result: no v0 P&L, no v0 equity curve, no v0
  HALT/exit state, no prior-run outcome (paper or live) presented as
  informative about what to trade. (Under the single-program reality there is
  no sealed/live v0; this exclusion still binds any local prior run.)
- Any market data, price, news, or catalyst dated at or after the agent's
  go-live.
- Any operator-authored market opinion, instrument preference, directional
  language, catalyst hint, or GTA-6 narrative.

## What the corpus MAY contain

Neutral mechanical reference, and the no-edge research finding stated
neutrally (NO-EDGE.md). Nothing here expresses a market view, a preferred
instrument, a direction, or a catalyst thesis. The agent learns everything
current only from the dated perception payload (perceive()), never from this
corpus.
